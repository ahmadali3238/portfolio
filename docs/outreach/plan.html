🎯 The Outreach Engine

A machine that quietly finds companies who need exactly what you build, writes each of them a personal email, and helps you turn replies into clients — running mostly on autopilot.

100% free stack — no paid keys Paid tools: optional, off by default Your portfolio: 0% ban risk

1 The big idea in one line

Five simple stages, each one hands off to the next.

Find companies hiring for your skills → get the right person's email → write them a personal note → send it safely → follow up smartly. A row of data travels through all five stages automatically. You stay in control at the parts that matter (approving sends and replies).

3 Why these leads are the good ones

We don't email random companies. We email ones raising their hand right now.

A live job post = a real need + budget

If a startup just posted a "Next.js / AI engineer" role, they have the problem you solve and money set aside. That's the warmest cold lead there is.

We match their words to your work

Each lead is scored 0–100 on how well their stack matches yours. Low scores are dropped. Claude then cites the one project of yours that fits them best.

4 The honest bit about sending

You asked to send from a free Vercel domain — that isn't possible. A vercel.app address can't be set up to send real email (no DKIM/SPF/MX), so those messages bounce or hit spam. And "free + huge volume + safe" cold email doesn't exist anywhere — pick any three.
The good news: your portfolio doesn't send mail at all, so it's 100% safe no matter what. You start in Draft mode (free, zero risk): Claude writes the email, you click "Open in Gmail" and hit send. When you're ready to automate, a $1–10/year side domain unlocks safe auto-send — one tiny cost, everything else stays free.

"Send without opening Gmail" = Gmail Send mode. You create a Google app password once, and the dashboard's Send button mails it straight through your Gmail — no domain, no opening the app. Your portfolio domain is never in the sending path, so it still can't be flagged.

4.5 Running it entirely free (no paid keys)

The whole stack runs on free tiers. Paid tools (Claude, BetterContact) are wired in but switched off by default — you never have to spend.

✍️ Writing emails → free

Your site already has Groq and Gemini — both with generous free tiers. They draft every email and sort every reply. Cost: ₨0. (Claude can be switched on later if you ever want it — off by default.)

📧 Finding emails → free & proven

Email already in the post (≈29%, measured) + scraping the company's contact page (proven live on real leads). For your top 10–20 leads/week you can also run a free finder's browser extension by hand. No paid API, no ToS risk. (Note: Prospeo/Hunter/Apollo free tiers have no API — paid only.)

4.6 Who we target: the rich markets, not the cheap ones

Important twist: most "best country" lists rank where to hire cheap devs from — that's you. We flip it and chase where the buyers with budget are.

🥇 Tier 1 — hit hardest

🇺🇸 USA · 🇦🇪 UAE/Dubai · 🇬🇧 UK · 🇨🇦 Canada · 🇦🇺 Australia · 🇩🇪 Germany · 🇳🇱 Netherlands · any "remote-worldwide" company.
Biggest budgets, most AI demand. UAE is ~1h from you and loves hiring Pakistani devs.

🥈 Tier 2 — secondary

🇮🇪 Ireland · 🇸🇬 Singapore · Nordics · 🇨🇭 Switzerland · 🇸🇦 Saudi/Qatar · 🇳🇿 NZ · 🇮🇱 Israel.
Good budgets, remote-friendly.

⛔ Excluded — weak currency

India · Pakistan · Bangladesh · Nigeria · Egypt · Indonesia · Philippines · Vietnam · Argentina …
Weak local currency = unviable budgets. Hard-excluded — never emailed or enriched. Fully editable list.

Each lead is auto-scored by country, so your paid credits and emails go to the people most likely to pay well. Agentic-AI job postings jumped +985% — demand is everywhere; we aim it at the budgets.

4.7 Two kinds of people we contact

Not just companies hiring — also ones who aren't but clearly need you.

🔥 "Hiring signal" — warm

They just posted a job for your exact stack. Angle: "I saw you're hiring for X — I've shipped exactly that. Worth a chat before a full-time hire?"
From: HN, RemoteOK, We Work Remotely.

💡 "Prospect" — not hiring, still needs you

New startups, fresh product launches, businesses with weak/old sites. Angle: "I built X for companies like yours — here's a concrete idea for you."
From: YC, Product Hunt, local businesses. This widens your funnel a lot.

4.8 See every conversation in one place

You always know where each lead stands.

💬 Chat-style thread per lead

Click any company and see the whole back-and-forth in order — your first email, follow-ups, and their replies — with dates and status.

🧠 Auto reply handling

Replies are pulled from your Gmail automatically (IMAP), sorted by Claude/Groq (interested? objection? not now?), and a tailored follow-up is drafted for you.

Who received / engaged / replied: every email shows Sent → Delivered (no bounce) → Clicked (they visited your tracked link / mini landing page) → Replied — all free & first-party. (Open-tracking via pixel exists but is off by default — pixels hurt deliverability and report false opens.)

6 What you'll need to do

Almost everything is automated. Here's your short list.

  1. One-time: grab one free key — GROQ_API_KEY (writing emails). Emails are found free (in-post + site scrape); no finder key needed. (Supabase you already have.)
  2. Each day (~10 min): open the dashboard, skim the drafted emails, click "Open in Gmail" on the good ones, send.
  3. When someone replies: paste their reply — Claude sorts it and writes your follow-up. You approve.
  4. Optional later: buy a $1 side domain to flip on full auto-send.
